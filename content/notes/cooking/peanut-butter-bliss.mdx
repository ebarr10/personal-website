## Ingredients

- 1 cup Organic Vanilla Soy Non-Dairy Beverage
- 1/2 banana
- 1 tbsp peanut butter

Optional:

- Frozen banana for creamier texture

---

## Substitutions

- Replaced vanilla soy milk with 1 cup regular milk + splash of vanilla extract
- Used frozen banana (only banana available)

---

## Result

Thicker and creamier from the frozen banana.

Peanut butter and banana carried well, and the added vanilla kept the profile close to the original idea.
